SERVICES

Izone Ltd offers BPM Consulting and Data analytics services in the line of developing, discovering and analyzing business process solutions.

BPM Consultancy

Under BPM Consultancy we offer the following services:

  • We develop and document for your organization a process architecture and a process landscape.
  • We discover and document your business processes using BPMN 2.0 an industry standard.
  • We analyze your business processes using quantitative techniques including Simulation and ascertain costs pertaining to your processes.
  • We identify and document the shortcomings and weaknesses of your Business Processes in an Issue List.
  • We incorporate Business Processes improvements and map “TO-BE” Business Processes
  • We document and standardize Business Decisions using industry Standard Decision Modeling Notation.
  • We Automate the “TO-BE” Business Processes and Business Decisions using recognized BPM Platforms.
  • We monitor and Evaluate Business Processes in production to further improve on them using Dashboards, Process Analytics and Process Mining.
  • We assist organizations implement BPM Centers of Excellence and embark on a continuous Process Improvement Programme.
